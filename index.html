Transformation of the urban environment

Trade, which served as the center of gravity and, as a consequence, the creation of cities, almost at once passes into the virtual space. Possible urban consequences: change in urban stereotypes, transformation of urban infrastructures.

Exodus from cities

Secular trends show the continuous coagulation of humanity. The attraction of cities is determined by the opportunities provided for implementation. However, infocommunication technologies provide such an opportunity, without being tied to a single topos. The imposed demands of the new person of the digital society for ecology, including visual, suddenly open a reverse trend, namely the exodus from cities.

Problems of planning in urbanism

A qualitative leap in the rate of development and, as a consequence, multiple socio-technological transformations during one human life make it extremely difficult to plan the development of urban infrastructure not only in the long term, but already in the medium and even the short term.
